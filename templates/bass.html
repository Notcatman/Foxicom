Groove with the foundation of every band – the bass!

Why Learn Bass Guitar?

The bass guitar is the heartbeat of every band, providing rhythm and depth. Whether you want to play rock, jazz, funk, or pop, this course will help you master the groove and become a confident bassist.

What You’ll Learn

  • Bass guitar anatomy & tuning
  • Essential fingerstyle and picking techniques
  • Grooves, riffs, and walking bass lines
  • Reading tabs and basic music notation
  • Playing with a band and locking in with the drummer
  • Improvisation and soloing basics
  • Instrument care and setup

This course is perfect for beginners and those looking to add groove to their playing.
